# Getting Started

Signing up for a Nomod account is easy.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started.png" />
</Frame>

Download the Nomod App from the [Play Store](https://nomod.app/g) or [App Store](https://nomod.app/a) to get started. Simply enter your details, and you'll be up and running in a few minutes.

Nomod is currently available to businesses in the United Arab Emirates and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with more countries to be supported soon.

Continue reading this guide to get an overview of Nomod's features, integrations, and how to use them. You can also revisit the guide whenever you want to learn more about a specific feature. Happy Nomoding!
