# Getting Started

Signing up for a Nomod account is super easy.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/getting-started.png" />
</Frame>

Download the Nomod App from the [Play Store](https://nomod.app/g) or [App Store](https://nomod.app/a) to get started. Enter your details and get up and running in a few minutes.

Nomod is available to businesses in the United Arab Emirates and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. More countries will be available in the future.

Continue reading this guide to get an overview of Nomod's features, integrations, and how to use them. You can also come back whenever you want to learn more about a feature. Happy Nomoding!
